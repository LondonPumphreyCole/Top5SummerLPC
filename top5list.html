Top 5 Things About my Summer in 2017

  1. Hurricane Harbor (Waterpark, obviously)

    2. The highlight of my summer, the nice beginning, the Hurricane Harbor!

    I went with a few friends, went on a waterslide crazier than a feminist on CNN,

    and I learned how to use the Lazy River to travel across the entire park!

  2. 4th of July Party (Watched my cousin get hurt by being stupid!)

    3. For the second best part of my Summer, I chose the 4th of July Party!

    After the first hours of boredom from being forced to do nothing but read Hooked stories and

    watch some dumb kids repetitively fail at Minecraft, my cousins finally arrived!

    When they arrived we had a great deal of fun. We had a water balloon war,

    we played Football, we did a lot of fun things and later at night, I watched a firework be lit up close,

    and thats when my cousin got hurt. At least it was nothing bad enough to go to the doctor about...

  3. Pool Party

    4. Before any of those but wasn't so fun, was a Pool Party hosted by the LDS Church out of all things (my Summers get real sad...)

    First coming in I thought it would be fun, we did a longest diving contest, some idiot tried to pull my trunks off,

    but I ended up catching him, offending him and making him so embarrassed he had to leave. Yeah that's the highlight of the party... (I told you, my summers get real sad!)

  4. Trip to Running Springs!

    5. Enough with that cringy embarrassment, onto my first and only trip during Summer! I went to Running Springs!

    During Running Springs we got to stay with my aunt and we went hiking, played some weird games, I finally figured out how to use snapchat (Yeah I felt real stupid),

    and I caught some real nice photos up there!

  5. Overwatch Summer Events!

    6. Onto my least favorite and most stressful part of Summer that STILL hasn't ended, Overwatch Summer!

    The stress of that is Lucioball, the most obnoxious part of the game with my worst character, Lucio.

    In Lucioball, you're basically playing Rocket League but instead of Cars or whatever you use, you get to use Lucio in the game.

    But if that was it, I would just avoid Overwatch till it's over. But that's not all... I get to deal with the high rarity and low luck with Loot Boxes!

    I really wanted Sporty Tracer, Biker Reaper, and Grillmaster: 76 but what do I get? None of those!

      Overall, my Summer was pretty good! I went to Running Springs, I went to Hurricane Harbor, and I had the greatest 4th in a LONG time!

      Yes, I had a bad time at my only pool party, I hated the Overwatch Summer Event I looked forward to, but I still had a great time with everything else on my list!
